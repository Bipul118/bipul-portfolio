The Engineer Behind the Code

I'm a B.Tech ECE student who refused to pick just one discipline. I move between Verilog and Python, between logic gates and neural networks, finding the most interesting problems where hardware meets software.

My current obsession: how AI can reshape semiconductor design — from ML-assisted RTL verification to generative architectures. When I'm not designing circuits or training models, I'm grinding LeetCode in C++ and building stock market analytics tools that turn raw data into trading signals.

I target roles where precision matters at every abstraction layer — from CMOS gate sizing at NVIDIA or AMD, to AI infrastructure at Google DeepMind or OpenAI.
